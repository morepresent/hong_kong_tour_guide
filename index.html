Visit Hong Kong

Enjoy a blend of Eastern and Western cultures and a cuisine haven in this vibrant international financial hub.

Top Three activities to do at Hong Kong

Take the Peak Tram to Victoria Peak

Enjoy a ride on the historic Peak Tram and get breathtaking views of Hong Kong’s skyline, Victoria Harbour, and the surrounding islands from Victoria Peak. It’s one of the best photo spots in the city!

Explore Temple Street Night Market

Wander through Temple Street Night Market in Kowloon. You can enjoy street food, shop for souvenirs, and experience local culture. It’s lively, colorful, and full of energy!

Visit Hong Kong Disneyland

Spend a magical day at Hong Kong Disneyland on Lantau Island. It’s perfect for families and Disney fans. You can enjoy rides, shows, parades, and meet your favorite Disney characters.
